About Us

Founded in 2019 by Yuumi Mortimer, NEW VISION Studio is a boutique creative house based in Miami. We combine fashion aesthetics, strategic marketing, and forward-thinking design to redefine how modern brands communicate their identity.

Our Expertise

We specialize in fashion design, brand storytelling, and creative consulting. Our work bridges culture, commerce, and creativity—bringing innovative ideas to life through visual strategy, product design, and marketing execution.
